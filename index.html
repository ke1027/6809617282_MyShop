About Us

RISE COFFEE is a Progressive Coffee Space that aims to make Specialty Coffee more accessible to everyone. We believe that "Everyday Coffee" should be high-quality, easy to access, and suited to the urban lifestyle. Our goal is to redefine Third Wave Coffee to promote a more inclusive coffee culture, from simple coffee stories to complex ones, all of which we use to build our community.
